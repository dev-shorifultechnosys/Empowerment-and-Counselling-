About InUnity

Rooted in Community, Growing Together

Like the ancient Baobab tree that provides shelter, nourishment, and strength to entire communities, InUnity stands as a pillar of support for individuals and families seeking healing and empowerment.

Founded in 2018, we've dedicated ourselves to creating safe, inclusive spaces where diverse voices are heard, trauma is understood, and every person can discover their inherent power to heal and thrive. Our approach honors cultural identities, celebrates diversity, and recognizes that true transformation happens through community connection.

Featured Programs

Transformative Community Programs

Explore our signature programs designed to build resilience, foster deep connection, and inspire meaningful change in individuals and communities.

Youth Program

Youth Arts & Healing

A creative expression program for young people ages 13-24 to process experiences and build confidence through art, music, dance, and storytelling in a supportive peer environment.

  • Weekly creative workshops and art therapy sessions
  • Mentorship from professional artists and counselors
  • Annual youth art exhibition and performance showcase
Enroll Now
Women's Program

Women's Empowerment Circle

A supportive space for women to connect, share, and grow together, focusing on topics of resilience, leadership, self-care, and navigating life transitions with strength and grace.

  • Bi-weekly facilitated discussion circles
  • Leadership development and skills training
  • Networking and community-building events
Join Circle
Family Program

Family Roots Project

A comprehensive program designed to strengthen family bonds and improve communication through guided activities, family counseling, and culturally-responsive therapeutic interventions.

  • Family therapy sessions with licensed counselors
  • Interactive family bonding activities and retreats
  • Parenting workshops and child development resources
